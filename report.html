Report & Submit Evidence

Three ways to build the record: report your case, add photos of your child, and archive the court documents that prove what happened.

How It Works

Step 1 — Report the case. Start with the case report form. This creates the case record that everything else links to.

Step 2 — Add your child. Once your case is listed, submit photos of your child with your consent to publish.

Step 3 — Archive the documents. Upload court decisions, Hague rulings, police reports, and custody orders, linked to your case.

Before you submit:
  • Nothing is published automatically. Every submission is reviewed and verified by our team before it appears on this site.
  • Cases are published only with supporting legal documents (court records, custody orders, police reports).
  • Photos of children are published only with the consent of the child or their parent.
  • Please redact personal information you do not want public (addresses, phone numbers, financial details) before uploading documents.
  • By submitting, you confirm the information is true to the best of your knowledge and that you accept responsibility for its accuracy.
