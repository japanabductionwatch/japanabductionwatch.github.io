Report a New Abduction Case

Submit details about a parental child abduction case involving Japan. Please provide as much information as possible.

Before you submit:
  • Cases are published only after review, with supporting legal documents (court records, custody orders, police reports).
  • Photos of children are published only with the consent of the child or their parent.
  • By submitting, you confirm the information is true to the best of your knowledge and that you accept responsibility for its accuracy.

Wrongly listed?

If you or your legal team believe you are wrongly listed on this site, please use the same form to request a formal review, or contact us by email.
